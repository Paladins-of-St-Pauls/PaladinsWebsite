WE ARE THE PALADINS OF ST PAUL'S!

BUILDING ENGINEERS

The Paladins of St Paul's are a rookie FTC team from St Paul's College, Gilles Plains, South Australia. We are working hard to learn all we can about the FIRST family of programs, meet teams from all around the world and promote STEM. We are determined to become outstanding ambassadors for FIRST and true gracious professionals.
